================================================================================
TASK 1 — BUSINESS DIRECTION & PORTFOLIO DECISION
================================================================================

Using the technical pack + shared business context above, set our overall direction.

## Do this

1. **Generate a LARGE, diverse menu of distinct routes to revenue/reputation.** Cover
   (a) leveraging each existing asset — Poof, Bounded, Bind, Smallhours, Ratri,
   cutscene.video, the Robbie engagement; (b) net-new products/harnesses (including the
   tennis simulator and AI-investment-desk ideas, plus ones you invent); (c) hybrid /
   portfolio plays. Include fast quick-wins (revenue in weeks) AND big swings.

2. **For each route:** the concrete step-by-step of *how it happens*; what's needed
   (people/skills, dollars, tokens/AI-loops, time, external deps, hardware, partners,
   legal); how much is **automatable vs needs our 4 people or manual work**; and how
   hands-off it can realistically be.

3. **Quantify like we're buying an outcome:** cost (cash + your-compute), expected
   payoff, **time-to-first-dollar**, time-to-meaningful-revenue, risk, and the signal
   that says kill vs double down. State assumptions and unknowns explicitly.

4. **Score and rank** all routes (expected value, speed, leverage of our specific
   assets, how autonomously you can run them).

## End in action, with conviction

- **A recommended portfolio, decided:** exactly what we **start now**, what we
  **queue**, what we **drop** — and why. Don't hedge.
- **A 30 / 60 / 90-day action plan.** Each action tagged with an owner:
  `[one of our 4]`, `[autonomous Fable loop]`, or `[manual/contractor]`, plus its
  cost and the outcome it must hit.
- **A budget allocation** of cash + compute across the portfolio (out of our $2.1M and
  ~23-month runway), with a hard cap per bet and the kill/double-down metric for each.
- **The single "north-star" metric** we should manage the whole company to for the
  next 90 days. Assume we act on your call.
