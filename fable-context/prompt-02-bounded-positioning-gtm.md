================================================================================
TASK 2 — IF BOUNDED IS THE ANSWER: POSITIONING & GO-TO-MARKET
================================================================================

Using the technical pack + shared business context above, assume we bet on **Bounded**
as our primary product. Tell us how to position and market it to win.

## Do this

1. **Sharp positioning & category.** One-sentence positioning, the category we own or
   create, and the **wedge vs Convex / Supabase / Firebase** — especially the
   agent-era angle ("the backend you and your AI can trust": proofs as the trust
   primitive that lets powerful, fallible agents write/spend/store safely). Be honest
   about the maturity gaps in the pack (e.g. BACKEND-004, `bound` unproven) and turn
   the ScopeLimits honesty into a differentiator.
2. **ICP(s) & ranking.** Who buys first and why (e.g. agent-app builders, indie devs,
   vibe-coding platforms, fintech/game/multi-tenant teams). Pick the beachhead.
3. **Messaging & naming.** Homepage narrative (note: our live site's README is stale
   and some sections are missing — see the pack), taglines, the proof/anti-cheat/money
   stories, and 3 **killer honest demos** we can build THIS WEEK that make people feel
   the "can't go wrong" claim.
4. **Distribution.** The concrete launch motion: dev channels, content, Show HN /
   Launch, docs/DX, design partners, community, and any paid tests worth running.
5. **Pricing check.** Whether live pricing (Free $0 / Pro $25; provider+5%; Pay 1%)
   fits this ICP, and any packaging changes.

## End in action, with conviction

- **The exact homepage rewrite direction** (section-by-section) to match the built
  product.
- **A 2-week launch checklist** and a **30/60/90-day GTM plan**, each item tagged
  `[one of our 4]` / `[autonomous Fable loop]` / `[manual/contractor]`, with cost.
- **The 3 demos to build now**, each with the policy.json sketch, the 60-sec script,
  and the one screenshot/GIF that sells it.
- **The go/no-go signals** (activation, retention, design-partner conversion) that tell
  us Bounded is or isn't the horse. Decide; don't hedge.
